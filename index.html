antique cars: def

An antique car is a classification that is often set by state law. States often have a special type of license plate for these cars. For that reason they set rules stating what qualifies as "antique." In most cases it is a car that's manufactured 1975 or earlier (>45 years old). Generally the car should be maintained in a way that keeps it true to the original manufacturer specifications
